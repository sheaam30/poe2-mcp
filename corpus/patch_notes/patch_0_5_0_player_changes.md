# Patch 0.5.0 — Player & Mechanic Changes

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

## Defense and Survivability

- Bleed Movement Penalty Removed: Bleed damage no longer increases while moving
- Runic Ward Integration: Adds to starting Honour in Trial of the Sekhemas
- Terminology Change: "Defences" now explicitly refers to armor, evasion, energy shield

## Leech Mechanics

- Single Instance Limit: Only highest recovery rate leech instance applies at a time
- Damage Cap: Hits over 40,000 treated as 40,000 for leech calculation; scaling maintained

## Deflection Formula

New formula: "150*(1 - A/(A + 0.12*D))" with 95% cap, where A = attacker accuracy, D = deflection rating

## Other Mechanics

- Heavy Stun Delay: Parry, shield block, resonating shield no longer extend decay
- Melee Splash Limit: Can apply once per damaging area per attack
- Minion Removal Priority: Newer minions removed first when weapon swapping
- Trinity Buff: Renamed from Resonance to Affinity
- Archon Buffs: No longer lost on attack (except elemental archon)
- Culling Strike Threshold: 35% of life (from 30%)
- Monster Leech Resistance: Starts later; lower at every level; uniform across rarities

## New Ascendancy Classes

- Martial Artist (Monk): Masters of illusions; uses rune sockets and illusory bells
- Spirit Walker (Huntress): Commands spirits aligned with Stag, Owl, or Bear

## New Features

- Fragment Stash Tab: New stash for fragments, inscribed ultimatums, baryas, tablets
- Build Guides: Download .build files showing passive allocations, ascendancy choices, gem recommendations
- Quick Trade Search: Shift-Alt click items to populate trade filters with pricing data
- Party Revive: Quickly revive fallen party members after defeating boss
- Dialogue Skip: Press Escape to skip NPC dialogue

## Campaign Replayability

- Directional Markers: Added to show progression path for experienced players
- Area Shortening: Several areas compressed for faster playthrough
- Dreadnaught Consolidation: Removed "The Dreadnaught Vanguard"; Act 2 boss now in "The Dreadnaught"
- Act 3 Reordering: Area progression rearranged for smoother flow
- Monster Density Reduction: Second half and Interludes have lower density
