# Patch 0.5.0 — Ascendancy & Passive Tree Changes

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

## Ascendancy Changes

### Acolyte of Chayula
- Into the Breach: Leech increased to 20% maximum life/mana (from 15%)

### Blood Mage
- Vitality Siphon: Increased to 20% spell damage leech (from 10%)

### Chronomancer
- Rapid River replaced by "Now and Again": Spells gain 20% echo/repeat chance
- Phased Form: New passive granting 30% damage reduction with 4-second delayed damage replay
- Temporal Rift: Reduced cast time to 0.1 seconds; removes Phased Form damage
- Inevitable Agony Rework: No longer curse; 2-second cooldown life loss effect; 25% damage conversion; 12-second duration
- Sands of Time: 1-60% increased skill speed (from cast speed)

### Gemling Legionnaire
- Crystalline Potential replaced by "Essence of Virtue": Grants protective gemstone motes accumulating over time

### Pathfinder
- Overwhelming Toxicity: 50% less poison duration (from 35%)
- Running Assault: 30% less movement speed penalty (from 50%)

### Witchhunter
- Obsessive Rituals: 50% less armor/evasion (from 35%)

## Passive Tree Changes

### New Content
- 19 Companion Passives: New skills themed around companion mechanics
- 9 Life Recoup Speed Passives: Dedicated life recovery nodes
- Archon of Undeath Cluster: New notable passive area

### Keystone Modifications
- Ancestral Bond: Places totems cost nothing; totem reserve 75 spirit; doubles totem limit
- Trusted Kinship: 30% more companion reservation efficiency; 20% less non-companion reservation efficiency
- Vaal Pact: 50% more life leeched; 67% less leech speed; cannot recover except from leech
- Reformed Barrier: Cluster deleted

### Notable Passive Adjustments
- Fortified Aegis: 100% increased shield defenses
- Arcane Mixtures: 10% cast speed with recent mana flask
- Chakra of Breathing: 20% faster ES recharge start
- Covering Ward: 25 ES on block; 12% block chance
- Critical Overload: 15% increased critical spell damage bonus
- Core of the Guardian: 30% block chance; 20% reduced maximum ES
- Fast Metabolism: 40% increased damage while leeching
- Goring: 3% increased maximum life; 20% increased life leech
- Shatter Palm: 20% increased critical damage bonus
- Stormcharged: Penetrate 8% resistances; 5% attack/cast speed with elemental skills
- Voracious: 15% increased attack speed while leeching
- Wide Barrier: 30% increased block chance; 20% reduced armor
- Wyvern's Breath: 40% increased elemental ailment application

### Minor Adjustments
- Starting Spell Damage: 10% at witch start (from 8%)
- Energy Shield Recharge Start: Small passives now 6% (from 15%)
- Life Leech Passives: Small passives now 8% (from 10%)
- Banner Area of Effect: 12% on small passives (from 15%)
