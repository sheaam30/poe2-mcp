# Patch 0.5.0 — Item, Rune, Currency & Unique Changes

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

## Currency & Rarity Changes
- Greater/Perfect Currencies: Made rarer; transmutation/augmentation significantly rarer
- Greater Orbs: Minimum level 44 (from 55); begin dropping in Act 4
- Divine Orbs: More common
- Corruption Changes: Multiplies modifier values instead of randomizing beyond range
- Omen of Corruption: No longer obtainable
- Campaign Essences: Increased quantity; reduced Infinite essence proportion

## Armor/Defense Values
- Armor Values: 33% more at level 65, tapering to 15% at level 80+
- Evasion Values: 33% more at level 65, tapering to 15% at level 80+
- Energy Shield: 8% more at level 65, unchanged at level 80+

## Weapon Changes
- Weapon Range: One-hand maces 1.3 (from 1.1); two-hand maces 1.5 (from 1.3); quarterstaves 1.4 (from 1.3)
- Bow Attack Speed: Obliterator/Artillery 1.1 APS (from 1.15)
- Warden/Guardian Bow Implicit: 25-35% chain (from 20-30%)

## Skill Level Modifiers on Items
- Two-Handed Melee/Projectile: +5 at tier 1 (from +7); all tiers adjusted
- One-Handed Melee/Projectile: +4 at tier 1 (from +5); all tiers adjusted
- Quiver Projectile Levels: +1 (from +2)
- Higher Level Gate: Skill gem levels require level 18+ to roll

## Rune Changes
- Body Runes: +30/45/60 life in armor (from +20/30/40); 3/4/5% physical leech in weapons (from 2/2.5/3%)
- Mind Runes: +20/30/40 mana in armor (from +15/25/35); 2/3/4% mana leech (from 1.5/2/2.5%)
- Rebirth Runes: 15/25/35 life per kill in weapons (from 10/20/30); 0.35/0.4/0.45% life regen in armor
- Inspiration Runes: 10/20/30 mana per kill (from 8/16/24); 20/25/30% mana regen in staves/wands
- Stone Runes: 20/30/40% stun buildup in weapons; +50/75/100 stun threshold in armor
- Vision Runes: +60/90/120 accuracy in weapons; 8/12/16% flask recovery in armor
- Elemental Runes (Desert/Glacial/Storm): +10/14/18% resistances in armor (from +10/12/14%)
- Attribute Runes: +6/9/12 to attribute (from +6/8/10)
- Lady Hestra's Rune: Cold damage vs chilled/frozen (removed flat damage)
- Thane Leld's Rune: Lightning damage vs shocked (removed flat damage)
- Thane Myrk's Rune: Fire damage vs ignited (removed flat damage)
- Lesser/Rebirth/Greater Rebirth Runes: ES recharge reduced significantly (4%/6%/8% from 12%/15%/18%)

## Unique Item Changes

### Major Reworks
- Atziri's Acuity Gloves: 150-200% defenses; +100-150 life; 10% physical leech; Herald of the Royal Queen skill
- Husk of Dreams (renamed Reverie): Grants Rite of Restoration; life flasks apply constantly; cannot use flasks
- Svalinn Shield: Cast on block skill costs nothing; +50-100 Runic Ward

### Notable Unique Changes
- Ab Aeterno Boots: 200-250% defenses; unaffected by slows while sprinting
- Blackflame Ring: Ignite deals chaos; withered doesn't expire on ignited enemies; 80-100% ignite magnitude
- Blistering Bond Ring: Fire contributes to bleeding; bleeding deals fire damage
- Brass Dome: 500-600% armor (from 700-800%)
- Carnage Heart Amulet: 25-50% damage while leeching
- Chober Chaber Mace: +2-3 minion skill levels
- Death's Harp Bow: +250-330% additional arrow chance (from 3 guaranteed arrows)
- Elevore Helmet: 1 charm charge per second (from 0.5); +1-2 charm slots
- Glowswarm Ring: Mana flask grants 100% guard for 4 seconds
- Levinstone Ring: Lightning skills chain +1 (removed level bonus)
- Megalomaniac Jewel: Drops identified
- Plaguefinger Gloves: Cannot inflict elemental ailments
- Radiant Grief Helmet: 200 base fire damage (from 100) for presence ignite
- Soul Mantle Body Armor: +75 spirit (removed totem life reduction)

### New Unique Items
44 new unique items added including Berek's Grip, Mageblood, Loreweave, Split Personality, and Voices.

## Idol Changes
- All Idols: Now socket in sceptres
- Fox Idol: Limit 1; other idols grant bonded; +5% skill quality
- Rabbit Idol: Limit 1; 12% item rarity; 15% spirit in sceptres
- Stag Idol: Limit 1; 15% projectile fork; 30% additional projectile on fork
- Atmohua's Soul Core: Ailment threshold 15% ES; stun threshold 15% ES

## Jewel Changes
- Ruby Jewels: +1-2 max rage (from +1)
- Sapphire Jewels: 7-13% chaos damage (from 6-12%)
- ES Recharge Modifiers: Converted from "faster start" to flat recharge rate across suffixes
- Cast Speed Rings/Amulets: No longer shared with foci/wands; higher roll levels
- Instant Leech Desecrated: No longer rolls
