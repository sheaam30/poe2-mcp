# Patch 0.5.0 — The Runes of Aldur League

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

The new league introduces a crafting mechanic centered on Remnants that allow players to craft items using Runic Recipes. Key features include:

- Remnant Crafting: Find Remnants with 2-10 slots; combine runeshapes to craft items and face enemy encounters
- Verisium Currency: New metal currency dropped by Remnant monsters
- Runic Ward Defense: New defense type that activates at 1 life, regenerates independently
- Runeforging System: Add Runic Ward to armors using Verisium (no downside below level 55)
- 13 Alloy Currency Items: Unlock through Remnant encounters to add crafted modifiers
- Unique Verisium Runeforging: Upgrade unique weapon/armor bases below level 55
- Ancient & Mythical Runes: Powerful bonuses specific to weapon types
- 21 Kalguuran Skills: Including Animus Exchange, Explosive Transmutation, Skyfall
- 8 Kalguuran Supports: Including Concussive Runes, Healing Runes
- Ocean Exploring: Logbook mechanic transformed into ocean exploration with islands
- New Hub: "The Ruins of Kingsmarch" southeast of starting location
- Pinnacle Boss: Defeat Olroth to access new endgame content
- Challenge League: Earn Knight of Aldur Armor Set pieces for completing challenges
