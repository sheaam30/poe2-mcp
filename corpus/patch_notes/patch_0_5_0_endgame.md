# Patch 0.5.0 — Endgame Overhaul

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

## Origins of Divinity Storyline

New endgame narrative involving traversing the Atlas to enter a Fortress and activate Gates:

- Fortress Maps: Grant Atlas Passive Tree points (replaces previous progression method)
- Expanded Atlas Tree: 300+ nodes for deeper specialization
- Ancient Modifiers: 40+ modifiers with chance to appear on maps outside fortress
- Gateway Maps: Access different fortress sections
- Precursor Tower Maps: New map types outside the fortress
- Arbiter of Divinity Boss: New pinnacle encounter
- Alternative Completion: Kill Arbiter 5 times to complete fortress sections

## Masters of the Atlas

New endgame progression system with asymmetric bonuses:

- Three Masters: Jado (Order of the Djinn), Hilda (Monster Hunter), Doryani (Science)
- 12 Nodes per Master: Select 4 at a time, swappable between maps
- Mission-Based Unlocks: Perform tasks to unlock new rows

## Delirium

- New Hub: "The Withered Willow" southwest of starting location
- Delirium Progress Bar: Shows depth progression and time remaining
- New Encounters: Colored mirror shards trigger special encounters
- Loathsome Mire: New sub-area dropping amulet bases with instilled notables
- Liquid Emotions: Craft additional mods on jewels like greater essences
- Grand Mirrors: Spawned after completing mirrors; duplicate map bosses
- Trial of Madness: New atlas mechanic with locked Simulacrum at 100% deliriousness
- Simulacrum Update: Now 7-wave encounter; completion grants key for pinnacle boss
- Ancient Emotions: New currency for Timelost Jewel crafting

## Breach

- New Hub: "The Monastery of the Keepers" south of starting location
- Stabilised Breach Mechanic: Progress bar triggers new challenge at 100%
- New Boss: Vruun, Marshal of Xesht
- Genesis Tree Crafting: Consume Wombgifts and Hiveblood to create rings, amulets, belts, currency
- 6 New Ring Bases, 4 New Amulet Bases, 4 New Belt Bases exclusive to Genesis Tree crafting
- New Catalyst Set: 12 catalysts adding quality modifiers to jewels
- Fortress Boss Pair: Tul and Esh grant key for pinnacle boss access

## Ritual

- New Hub: "Caer Tarth" west of starting location
- Ritual Rewards: All items are uniques or omens
- King in the Mists: Spend unused tribute to gain audience; defeating grants The Head of the King
- Rite of the Nameless: 5-map continuous ritual with accumulating monsters
- Queen in the Mists: New boss dropping 3 corrupted idols

## Abyss

- Large Cracks: Appear on atlas; completing closes them with guaranteed Abyssal Depths boss
- Kulemak's Invitation: Always allocated to map owner
- Level Restrictions: Abyss omens no longer drop below level 65

## Fate of the Vaal (Now Core)

- Campaign Integration: 6 Ancient Beacons in Act 3, 6 more in Interludes
- Atziri's Temple: Located on atlas at Lira Vaal (north east of start)
- Room Mechanics: Tier 4 upgrades available; rooms convert to paths instead of deleting
- Crystal Storage: Increased to 60
- 4 Infuser Types: Armourer's, Blacksmith's, Arcanist's, Catalysing variants

## Other Endgame Changes

- Atlas Reset: New endgame storyline requires Origins of Divinity completion for atlas points
- Pinnacle Boss Versions: Quest and Infinite Farm versions; fragments removed
- Waystone Identification: Must identify before activating in map device
- Waystone Bonuses: Provide monster effectiveness, pack size, rarity, or waystone drops
- Pack Size: Now includes chance for additional rare monsters
- Monster Rarity Stat: New stat affecting magic/rare chance and modifier application
- Expedition: Temporarily disabled from standard leagues pending Runes of Aldur integration
