# Patch 0.5.0 — Skill & Support Changes

Source: https://www.pathofexile.com/forum/view-thread/3932540

## Projectile Behavior
- Fixed Projectile Skills: Now benefit from projectile count modifiers via supports like Ricochet III
- Crossbow Ammunition: Base cost 0 instead of disabled; affected by cost modifiers

## Command Skills
- Movement Speed Penalty: Reduced to 50% during skill use (from 70%)

## Specific Skill Changes

- Align Fate: Cooldown recovery now applies to visage frequency
- Ancestral Warrior Totem: Removed 0.6-second hidden delay; now 50% of skill attack time
- Bonestorm: No longer has Sustained tag
- Boneshatter: Quality grants 0-20% attack speed (from 0-30%)
- Comet: Reduced damage slightly; Fire-Infused adjusted
- Cull the Weak: Can't be evaded; 109-281% damage; 75% attack speed; mana cost 8-42
- Defiance/Dread/War Banner: No movement speed penalty during use; 6-meter base radius (from 4.5)
- Earthquake: Aftershock 184-666% damage (from 160-580%)
- Flame Breath: Added energy gain penalty
- Fortifying Cry: Single stack consumed per shockwave; cannot hit same enemy multiple times
- Fragmentation Rounds: Quality grants +0-2 fragments (from damage bonus)
- Freezing Salvo: 34-68% more chill magnitude (from 56-107%)
- Gathering Storm: Perfect Timing explosion deals 564-869% damage; creates shocked ground; 50 shockwave limit
- Ghost Dance: Interval now cooldown-based; gains shroud on hit; regenerates ES on shroud loss
- Grim Feast: Resurrection has 1-second cooldown
- Ice Shot: Ice Shards no longer have 25% more freeze buildup
- Ice Strike: Quality 0-20% attack speed (from 0-10% more)
- Lightning Arrow: Multiple beams cannot chain to same target
- Magma Barrier: 5-7 damage per 15 defenses (from 6-8)
- Oil Barrage: 18% mana cost reduction; Empowered uses initial cost per second
- Parry: Area of effect adjusted; no bonus distance
- Poisonburst Arrow: 3-second poison duration; quality 0-10% magnitude (from 0-20%)
- Pounce: 6-5.1 second cooldown (from 4.9-4)
- Rend: Fixed level scaling bug; adjusted damage accordingly
- Resonating Shield: 5-7 damage per 15 armor
- Rolling Magma: Chains 3-5 times (from 2-4)
- Rolling Slam: +1 second attack time; reduced damage for both slams
- Shattering Spite: 50% less ailment magnitude
- Shield Wall: 5-7 damage per 15 armor
- Snipe: Reduced base damage; 50% unique damage bonus; 3-meter radius
- Spell Totem: Can move while casting
- Tame Beast: 40-84% more beast damage; summons immediately if spirit available
- Tempest Bell: Can be ancestrally boosted; 3 max bells (from 1)
- Time of Need: 9.3-8.1 seconds (from 11.3-10.1)
- Toxic Growth: 5 pustules; 5-pustule limit (from 12)
- Volcano: 8% base crit chance (from 5%)
- Wing Blast: 25% more shockwave damage

## Support Changes

### Seal Standardization
- Salvo Support: 1 second per seal (from 2); 6 max seals (from 3); 1 projectile per seal (from 2)

### Specific Support Updates
- Advancing Storm: Support any storm skill
- Arjun's Medal: 25% kill blow load chance (from 100%)
- Bhatair's Vengeance: 1% damage per 2 rage (from 1 per rage); 15-second duration (from 20)
- Culmination II: 6-second combo loss (from 4); 10 max combo (from 20); 4% more damage per combo (from 2%)
- Dialla's Desire: +5% gem quality (from +10%)
- Infernal Legion I: 10% life cost (from 20%); 1.5-meter radius ignite
- Infernal Legion II: 10% life cost; 2-meter radius; +20% fire resistance
- Infernal Legion III: No longer obtainable
- Living Lightning: No longer replaces minions
- Overextend: No longer obtainable
- Rage I/II/III: Now support minion skills
- Ratha's Assault: 4 dodge bolts (from 5); 10% attack speed (from 20%)
- Refraction I/II: Grant allies deflection rating from evasion
- Uhtred's Augury: +2 levels with exactly 2 supports (from +3)
- Uhtred's Omen: +2 levels with exactly 1 support (from +3)
- Volt: 2 charges per meter (from 3); 1% damage per charge consumed (from 5% per 10)

### New Lineage Supports
19 new lineage supports including: Arbiter's Reach, Breachlord's Amalgam, Catha's Brilliance, and others themed around endgame bosses and mechanics.
